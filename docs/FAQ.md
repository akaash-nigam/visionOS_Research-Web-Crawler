## General Questions

### Q: What is Research Web Crawler?
**A:** Research Web Crawler is a comprehensive visionOS application providing powerful features and seamless user experience.

### Q: Is Research Web Crawler free to use?
**A:** Offers both free and premium tiers. Free includes core features; premium unlocks advanced functionality, additional storage, and priority support.

### Q: What platforms is it available on?
**A:** Currently available for visionOS. We evaluate expansion based on user demand.

### Q: How is my data protected?
**A:** All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices and comply with GDPR and CCPA.

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I create an account?
**A:**
1. Download and install Research Web Crawler
2. Open app and tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter email and create password
4. Verify your email
5. Complete profile setup

### Q: Do I need an account?
**A:** Some features available without account, but creating one unlocks full functionality and enables data sync.

### Q: How do I reset my password?
**A:**
1. On login screen, tap "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Follow link to create new password

### Q: Can I use the app offline?
**A:** Yes! Core features work offline. Data syncs automatically when back online.

## Features & Functionality

### Q: How do I sync data across devices?
**A:**
- Sign in with same account on all devices
- Enable sync in Settings > Data & Sync
- Data syncs automatically when connected
- Manual sync by pulling down to refresh

### Q: Can I export my data?
**A:** Yes, export in multiple formats:
1. Go to Settings > Data Management
2. Select "Export Data"
3. Choose format (JSON, CSV, PDF)
4. Data sent to email or saved to device

### Q: How do I share content?
**A:**
1. Select item to share
2. Tap share icon
3. Choose sharing method
4. Confirm and send

### Q: Can I customize appearance?
**A:** Yes! Options include:
- Dark/light mode
- Multiple themes
- Adjustable text size
- Layout preferences
- Notification settings

## Account & Privacy

### Q: How do I delete my account?
**A:**
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Scroll to bottom, tap "Delete Account"
3. Confirm decision
4. Account and data deleted within 30 days

**Note:** Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Q: What data is collected?
**A:** We collect only necessary data:
- Account info (email, username)
- Usage analytics (anonymized)
- App performance data
- User-generated content

See Privacy Policy: https://akaash-nigam.github.io/research-web-crawler/privacy

### Q: How do I control privacy settings?
**A:**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security
2. Review and adjust:
   - Data collection preferences
   - Analytics opt-in/out
   - Third-party sharing
   - Marketing communications

### Q: Is my data sold to third parties?
**A:** No, we never sell personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics, but it cannot be traced to individuals.

## Technical Issues

### Q: App keeps crashing. What should I do?
**A:**
1. Force quit and restart app
2. Check for updates
3. Restart device
4. Clear app cache
5. Reinstall if issues persist
6. Contact support if problem continues

### Q: Why isn't data syncing?
**A:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify you're signed in
3. Ensure sync enabled in Settings
4. Try manual sync
5. Check storage limits
6. Sign out and back in

### Q: App running slowly?
**A:**
- Close other apps
- Clear app cache
- Check device storage
- Update to latest version
- Restart device
- Check for system updates

### Q: Not receiving notifications?
**A:**
1. Check app notification settings
2. Verify device permissions
3. Ensure Do Not Disturb is off
4. Check in-app preferences
5. Reinstall if needed
