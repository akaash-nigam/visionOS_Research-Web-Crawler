## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection Statement

Research Web Crawler is committed to protecting your privacy.

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your usage
- We do NOT send analytics
- We do NOT upload your research data
- We do NOT require an account

**What IS Stored:**
- All your research data is stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync (coming in future update, opt-in only)
- Data stored in app-specific container
- Included in iCloud device backups (if enabled in iOS settings)

**Web Scraping:**
- When you scrape a URL, your device makes a direct request to that site
- No proxy servers - direct connection
- Rate limiting respects site policies (1 request per second per domain)
- Standard browser user agent

**Permissions:**
- Network: Used only for web scraping when you request it
- Storage: Your research data stored in app container

**Data Deletion:**
- Delete any source or project anytime
- Uninstalling the app removes all data
- No data remains on our servers (we don't have servers!)

**Third-Party Services:**
- We use no third-party analytics
- We use no third-party tracking
- We use no advertising networks

**Changes to Privacy Policy:**
- We will notify users of any changes via app updates
- Continued use constitutes acceptance
